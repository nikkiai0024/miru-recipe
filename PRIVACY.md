# Privacy Policy for ミルレシピ

**Last Updated: March 14, 2026**

## Overview

ミルレシピ is a recipe app that converts YouTube cooking videos into step-by-step recipes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect

**Locally Stored Data**
- Recipes you save are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
- No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not collect or store your location.
- We do not track your usage behavior.
- We do not share any data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

**YouTube Data API**
- ミルレシピ uses the YouTube Data API to retrieve video information (title, description, thumbnail) when you add a recipe via URL.
- No personal data is sent to YouTube beyond the video URL you provide.
- YouTube's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google AdMob**
- ミルレシピ displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob.
- AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization.
- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

**In-App Purchases**
- In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information.
- Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Children's Privacy

ミルレシピ does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
